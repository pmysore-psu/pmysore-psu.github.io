1. Potato fries


What youre gonna do is start by cutting your potatoes into fry shapes. So like rectangles.

They can be small, large, anyhow you like. Ideally, try to dry them off a bit.

I've heard some people drench them on cold water to prevent starch, so you can try that if you want but usually if I'm making fries i'm mad hungry.

So when I make these fries I put them on a sheet pan w/ a little drizzle of oil. i've been addnig this roasted garlic and herb seasoning plus some paprika. next time i get seasoning i'll buy old bay or something like that. gotta have that little drizzle of oil though. make sure you coat everything evenly, so nothing sticks (don't wanna clean a mess).

Put em in the oven at like 400-425 fahrenheit or so, for like 15ish minutes or so. It really depends on what you see and how you cut the potatoes. I generally take them out when I feel they're softish.

leave em to cool for a little and enjoy.
